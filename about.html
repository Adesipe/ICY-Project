The school was founded 2nd Feb. 1932 by two missions-The Anglican and Methodist Church. The school started with 55 boarder and 95 day students. The school was taken over by government during the second republic, and later returned to the original owners (Anglican & Methodist missions) in 2001. The school still operate both day and boarding.
